About

Who I Am

I'm an AI Systems Engineer specializing in building scalable, high-impact intelligent systems by designing autonomous multi-agent state machines and production-grade LLM pipelines. With 3+ years of experience, my core technical focus centers entirely on deterministic graph architectures and robust system orchestration.

My engineering approach bridges the gap between stochastic models and reliable, fault-tolerant software execution. Looking ahead, my vision is to transition these orchestration frameworks into advanced research and future studies centered on robotics intelligence, machine intelligence, and edge AI architectures.

When I'm not architecting core agent networks, you'll find me engineering open-source LLMOps observability utilities, exploring hardware-software co-design, or tracking the frontiers where autonomous digital workflows intersect with physical environments.
